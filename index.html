About Me

I'm a programmer driven by the intersection of tight gameplay mechanics and striking visual aesthetics. With over 5 years of experience in the games industry, I've had the pleasure of bringing both indie gems and AA titles to life.

My philosophy is simple: write clean code, build scalable systems, and never lose sight of the player's experience. When I'm not debugging a tricky physics interaction, you can find me analyzing mechanics in classic retro games or exploring algorithmic art.
